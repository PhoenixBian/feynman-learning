# Feynman Learning

AI-powered learning system. AI writes lectures from primary sources, then becomes a difficult listener — chasing jargon, flagging contradictions, blocking hand-waving. No test, no score. You decide when you've learned enough.

## Quick Start

```shell
/feynman-learning
```

Say "learn [topic]" with a file/URL, or say "demo" for a 5-minute trial run.

## How It Works

1. AI writes a lecture from your source material
2. You talk about it — AI pushes back in real-time
3. Go deeper: scenario testing (curiosity) or actual production (craft)
4. When you want more, AI suggests the next lecture direction

## Key Files

- `learning-sessions/{topic}/` — lectures, discussions, progress per topic
- `settings/learner-profile.md` — optional background for better lectures
